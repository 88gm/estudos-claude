KEY INSIGHTS & BUSINESS IMPLICATIONS
================================================================================

1. CUSTOMER SERVICE IS THE STRONGEST PREDICTOR OF CHURN
   --------------------------------------------------------
   • Effect size: 0.596 (LARGE)
   • Churned customers had 3.2 interactions vs 2.5
   • +28% more customer service contacts
   → IMPLICATION: High CS interactions indicate dissatisfaction
   → ACTION: Proactively address issues, improve product quality

2. LOW ENGAGEMENT IS A MAJOR CHURN INDICATOR
   ------------------------------------------
   • Viewing Hours: -16.6 hours less (20% decrease)
   • Binge Sessions: -1.5 sessions less (20% decrease)
   → IMPLICATION: Customers disengage before churning
   → ACTION: Implement early warning system for declining engagement
   → ACTION: Send personalized recommendations to re-engage users

3. SUBSCRIPTION TIER MATTERS
   --------------------------
   • Basic tier: 43.5% churn
   • Premium tier: 24.1% churn
   • Difference: 19.4 percentage points
   → IMPLICATION: Lower-tier customers more likely to churn
   → ACTION: Offer upgrade incentives with exclusive content
   → ACTION: Ensure basic tier has adequate value proposition

4. CONTENT PREFERENCES SHOW MODERATE IMPACT
   ------------------------------------------
   • Highest churn genre: Horror (52.3%)
   • Lowest churn genre: Documentary (25.9%)
   • Range: 26.4 percentage points
   → IMPLICATION: Genre preference has some correlation with churn
   → ACTION: Ensure content diversity across all genres
